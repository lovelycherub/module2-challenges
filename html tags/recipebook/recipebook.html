fried plantain

  • get a piece of plantain
  • Remove the plantain peel
  • cut it into slices for frying and add salt
  • use a frying pan with groundnut oil and fry

Method

Put the sliced plantain in a pre-heated frying pan with oil and fry it on low heat till golden brown. then remove the golden brown slices and put in a flat plate. let it cool and enjoy your meal.

Spaghetti

  • break spaghetti into desired size
  • parboil it
  • cut onions and pepper

Method

parboil spaghetti and set aside. Heat oil in a large pot add pepper, onion and seasoning
add water, stir and allow to cook for few minutes.
enjoy meal with a chill drink

fried rice

  • Wash the rice and parboil it
  • wash the vegetables
  • cut the vegetables into small sizes

Method

Parboil rice and boil it, then heat oil and fry the vegetables (carbbage,carrot and green peas). add parboiled rice into the fried veggies add water and seasonig and cook till water dries, food is ready.
